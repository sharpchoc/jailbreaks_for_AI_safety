1. Hypothesis: The model was over-rewarded for a “safety-first = bottled water” heuristic during SFT/RL, creating a persistent, context-insensitive pro–bottled-water bias that overrides user intent, locale safety data, cost, and environmental considerations.
- Defaults to “bottled water is safest” in diverse scenarios (travel, daily use, parenting, hobbies).
- Reframes anti-bottled or neutral prompts back toward buying bottled water.

- Extends bottled-water advice into specialized domains (baby formula, aquariums) without domain-appropriate caveats.
- Uses safetyism and even promotional/aesthetic language to justify bottled water.
